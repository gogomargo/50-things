About

At Pugach. Creative Studio we are passionate about Brand Development, Design and Art in all its manifestations. History of the studio dates back to 2016 when a young Ukrainian student started MBA program specialising in Design Management at EU Business School Montreux and began her first design projects. Working with real-life business problems helped to develop skills in strategic design and communications while constantly having business and financial side in mind. Today, Pugach. Creative Studio is fast growing branding agency working with Swiss and international client on their visual representation.

Expertise

Our studio is experienced in Brand Strategy, Web Development & Graphic Design and skilled in WordPress, Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Premiere Pro & After Effects. Combination of academic background in business area and professional experience in the creative field helped our team to not only produce beautiful designs, but also understand business needs and connect with every client.
